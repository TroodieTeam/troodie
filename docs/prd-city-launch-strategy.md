# Product Requirements Document: City Launch Strategy & Viral Playbook

## Executive Summary
Create a repeatable, viral city launch framework that builds local network effects through gamification, creator partnerships, and community challenges - turning each city into a self-sustaining growth engine.

## Problem Statement
- **Current Challenge**: Launching in new cities requires heavy marketing spend and slow organic growth
- **Opportunity**: Cities have natural community boundaries perfect for viral loops
- **Impact**: A successful city model can be replicated nationwide for predictable growth

## Solution Overview
A comprehensive city launch playbook combining the "Troodie Passport" discovery experience, creator-led content campaigns, community challenges, and local leaderboards to create FOMO and drive viral adoption within defined geographic markets.

## User Personas

### Local Creators
- **Profile**: Micro-influencers with strong local following (1K-20K)
- **Pain Points**:
  - Hard to monetize local influence
  - Limited brand partnership opportunities
  - Need fresh content ideas
- **Goals**:
  - Become the go-to local food expert
  - Grow following
  - Monetize influence

### Active Foodies
- **Profile**: Users who dine out 3+ times per week
- **Pain Points**:
  - Same restaurant rotation
  - FOMO on new spots
  - No trusted local recommendations
- **Goals**:
  - Discover hidden gems
  - Be first to new restaurants
  - Share discoveries

### Restaurant Owners
- **Profile**: Local restaurant operators
- **Pain Points**:
  - Competing with chains
  - Building local awareness
  - Reaching new customers
- **Goals**:
  - Drive foot traffic
  - Build loyal customer base
  - Understand local market

### Casual Users
- **Profile**: Occasional diners seeking recommendations
- **Pain Points**:
  - Overwhelmed by choices
  - Don't trust review sites
  - Want curated options
- **Goals**:
  - Quick reliable recommendations
  - Special occasions spots
  - Try trending places

## Core Features

### 1. Troodie Passport
**Description**: Curated collection of must-try restaurants in each city, gamified as a digital passport

**Components**:
- 30-50 hand-picked restaurants per city
- Categories: Hidden Gems, Date Night, Brunch Spots, Late Night, Local Legends
- Digital stamps for visiting/saving
- Progress tracking and badges
- Creator co-signs on each spot

**Passport Tiers**:
- **Explorer** (5 stamps): Unlock local creator content
- **Adventurer** (15 stamps): Early access to new restaurants
- **Ambassador** (30 stamps): Exclusive events and offers
- **Legend** (50 stamps): Creator status and monetization

**Gamification Elements**:
- Check-in verification (GPS/photo)
- Streak bonuses for consecutive days
- Friend challenges
- Limited-time stamp events
- Seasonal passports

**Technical Requirements**:
- Geofencing for check-ins
- Achievement system
- Progress persistence
- Social sharing integration

### 2. City Leaderboards
**Description**: Real-time rankings driving competitive discovery

**Leaderboard Types**:
- **Creators**: Most influential content
- **Explorers**: Most restaurants discovered
- **Restaurants**: Most saved this week
- **Neighborhoods**: Most active areas

**Ranking Factors**:
- Saves generated
- New restaurant discoveries
- Friend referrals
- Content engagement
- Check-in streaks

**Rewards**:
- Weekly winner spotlights
- Monthly prizes (gift cards, experiences)
- Annual city awards event
- Creator verification badges

**Technical Requirements**:
- Real-time scoring engine
- Fraud detection
- Historical tracking
- Public API for displays

### 3. Community Challenges
**Description**: Time-bound city-wide challenges driving specific behaviors

**Challenge Examples**:
- **Restaurant Week Rally**: Save 10 participating restaurants
- **Neighborhood Crawl**: Check into 5 SouthEnd spots
- **Hidden Gem Hunt**: Discover 3 restaurants with <50 saves
- **Creator's Choice**: Try 5 creator-recommended spots
- **New Opening Sprint**: First 100 to try new restaurant

**Challenge Mechanics**:
- Clear objectives and deadlines
- Progress bars and notifications
- Group challenges (compete with friends)
- Sponsor integration opportunities
- City vs. city competitions

**Rewards Structure**:
- Digital badges and achievements
- Restaurant credits/discounts
- Exclusive access/events
- Creator meet-and-greets
- Merchandise

**Technical Requirements**:
- Challenge engine
- Progress tracking
- Notification system
- Reward distribution

### 4. Local Creator Network
**Description**: Structured program for local creators to drive adoption

**Creator Tiers**:
- **Rising Star** (0-1K followers): Access to events
- **Local Voice** (1K-5K): Commission eligible
- **City Influencer** (5K-20K): Paid partnerships
- **Troodie Ambassador** (20K+): Equity/revenue share

**Creator Tools**:
- Content calendar templates
- Restaurant photography access
- Trending spot alerts
- Performance dashboard
- Co-marketing opportunities

**Monetization**:
- $5 per new user acquired
- $20 per restaurant claimed
- Sponsored content opportunities
- Event appearance fees
- Monthly retainers for top performers

### 5. Viral Mechanics
**Description**: Built-in features designed to drive organic sharing

**Sharing Triggers**:
- Passport milestones ("I just became a Charlotte Food Explorer!")
- Leaderboard achievements ("I'm #3 in SouthEnd!")
- Challenge completions ("Restaurant Week Challenge Complete ✓")
- Friend invites ("Beat my record of 25 restaurants")

**Social Proof Elements**:
- "3 friends saved this"
- "Trending in your neighborhood"
- "Only 5 people have discovered this"
- "Creator pick of the week"

**Referral Program**:
- Give $5, Get $5 restaurant credits
- Unlock passport spots for referrals
- Group challenge bonuses
- Creator referral codes

## Launch Playbook

### Pre-Launch (4 weeks before)
**Week -4: Foundation**
- Identify top 50 restaurants
- Sign 10 anchor restaurants
- Database and profile creation

**Week -3: Creator Recruitment**
- Identify 20 target creators
- Send partnership proposals
- Creator onboarding sessions

**Week -2: Content Creation**
- Creators visit restaurants
- Generate launch content
- Build buzz on social

**Week -1: Beta Access**
- 100 beta users (creators + VIPs)
- Collect feedback
- Fix critical issues

### Launch Week
**Day 1: Creator Blast**
- All creators post simultaneously
- Launch party at popular venue
- Press release to local media

**Day 2-3: Challenges Begin**
- Activate first city challenge
- Push notifications to beta users
- Social media advertising starts

**Day 4-5: Momentum Building**
- Leaderboard highlights
- Creator stories featuring users
- Restaurant spotlights

**Day 6-7: First Week Wrap**
- Celebrate top performers
- Announce week 2 challenges
- Share growth metrics

### Post-Launch Growth (Weeks 2-8)
**Weeks 2-4: Scaling**
- Weekly challenges
- Creator content cadence
- Restaurant onboarding

**Weeks 5-8: Optimization**
- A/B test challenge types
- Refine passport selections
- Expand creator network

## Success Metrics

### Launch Metrics (First 30 Days)
- **Users**: 1,000 active users
- **Creators**: 20 active creators
- **Restaurants**: 50 claimed profiles
- **Engagement**: 5 saves per user

### Growth Metrics (90 Days)
- **MAU**: 5,000 users
- **Viral Coefficient**: >1.2
- **Creator Content**: 100 posts/month
- **Restaurant Conversion**: 30% claim rate

### City Health Score
```
Score = (MAU × Engagement Rate × Creator Activity × Restaurant Participation) / City Population

Healthy: >0.1
Growing: 0.05-0.1
Struggling: <0.05
```
