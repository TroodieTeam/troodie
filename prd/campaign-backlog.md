# Troodie Campaign Backlog

This document outlines 12 ready-to-launch campaigns for Troodie's creator marketplace, designed to showcase different content types, budgets, and creator engagement levels.

## Campaign Overview

Each campaign includes:
- **Budget per creator** (in USD)
- **Creator count** (target number of creators)
- **Content requirements** (specific deliverables)
- **Campaign duration** (typically 30 days)
- **Campaign type** (Troodie Direct, Partnership, Community Challenge)
